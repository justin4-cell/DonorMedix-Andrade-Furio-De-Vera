How DonorMedix Works

Our simple three-step process connects donors with recipients to ensure medicines reach those who need them most.

1

Donate Medicines

List your unused, unexpired medicines with photos, expiry dates, and location details.

2

Get Matched

Our system matches your donations with nearby recipients who need those specific medicines.

3

Make Impact

Coordinate safe pickup or delivery and help save lives while reducing medical waste.

Why Choose DonorMedix?

We provide a safe, verified platform for medicine donations with features designed for community trust and safety.

1

Verified Donations

All medicine donations are verified for expiry dates and authenticity before being listed.

2

Smart Search & Filters

Advanced search by medicine name, category, location, and expiry date to find exactly what you need.

3

Real-time Notifications

Get instant alerts when your donations are matched or when requested items become available.

4

Trust & Verification

User profiles with ratings, reviews, and verification badges help build trust within the community.

5

Secure Messaging

Built-in chat system allows safe communication between donors and recipients for coordination.

6

Mobile Responsive

Fully optimized for mobile devices, making it easy to donate and find medicines on the go.
