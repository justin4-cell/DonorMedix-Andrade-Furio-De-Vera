Donation Guidelines

  • Only donate medicines that are not expired or close to expiry
  • Ensure medicines are stored properly (temperature, humidity)
  • Include original packaging and labels when possible
  • Do not donate controlled substances or prescription-only medicines
  • Avoid donating opened liquid medicines or creams
